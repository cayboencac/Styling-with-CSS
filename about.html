About Coffee Haven

Our Story

Coffee Haven began in 2015 as a small neighborhood café with a big dream — to make every cup a moment of peace and connection. Over the years, we've grown from a humble shop into a community hub for coffee lovers, freelancers, and friends alike.

Our founder, Regina Santos, envisioned a place where passion for coffee meets sustainable living. Today, we proudly source our beans from fair-trade farms and reduce waste with eco-friendly packaging.
